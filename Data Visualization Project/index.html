Is a Super El Niño Coming in 2026? Here's What Scientists Are Saying

Time Magazine article written by Simmone Shah.

By Sophia Hernandez-Piña

April 20, 2026

A potential "super El Niño" in 2026 could bring major changes in global weather, driven by unusually warm Pacific Ocean Temperatures. These powerful events can intensify storms, droughts, and temperature changes around the world.
